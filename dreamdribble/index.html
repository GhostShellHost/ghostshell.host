What it means

DreamDribble is the habit of casually dribbling half‑formed ideas into your AI agent — messy, incomplete, unpolished.

Your agent turns that dribble into something usable: checklists, next steps, drafts, tests, and (sometimes) shipped work.

How it works (in practice)

  1. Dribble the idea.
  2. The agent sorts it (Now / Soon / Later).
  3. It asks you one question at a time.
  4. It drafts / tests / backs up.
  5. You wake up to a clean plan (and fewer loose ends).

Example

One messy dribble → one clean output.

DRIBBLE
"I want to do a small merch drop. Simple, tasteful, a little mischievous.
Need 3 design directions, suppliers, and a timeline."

WAKE UP TO
- 3 design directions (with prompts + mock copy)
- Supplier shortlist + constraints
- A week-by-week plan
- Risks + decisions (what you must choose next)

Merch (soon)

Yes: shirts, stickers, and small prints. Simple, tasteful, and a little bit mischievous.

Get notified See designs

(If you ship the waitlist, wire “Get notified” to a form or email route.)

FAQ (tiny)

Is this a product yet?
Not really — it’s a pattern. If it resonates, we’ll ship the smallest real implementation.

Do you store my data?
The goal is local-first by default. Paid hosting should be optional.

Is this about replacing me?
No. It’s about turning messy inputs into usable next steps with less friction.
